Page 02 — Why Join

Knowing grammar is easy.
Starting the conversation is the hard part.

✦ 01

100% English Zone

From wake-up to lights-out, every conversation happens in English. No shortcuts, no exceptions.

✦ 02

Real Native Speaker Facilitators

3 native facilitators plus 3 local mentors, right there with every tribe, every day.

✦ 03

The Amazing Linguistic Race

6 game stations — not lectures. Running, shouting, negotiating, all in English.

✦ 04

Certificate & Documentation

Campers go home with an official certificate, a highlight video, and brand new confidence.
